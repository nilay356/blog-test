MU20 — Global Youth Summit 2025

Asia’s Largest High School Career Conference

About the Event

MU20 is an international event that brings together thousands of high school students for leadership development, innovation showcases, and career-focused competitions.

With participation from schools across India and abroad, MU20 stands as Asia’s largest and most influential high school career summit.

Hosted By

The event is hosted at prestigious institutions such as Emerald Heights International School and is organized in partnership with NMIMS Indore and several other educational leaders.

What Students Gain

MU20 provides an incredible lineup of opportunities including:

  • Leadership & public speaking skill development
  • Career-focused workshops & expert-led sessions
  • Global exposure through collaborative challenges
  • Competitions in innovation, entrepreneurship, and problem-solving
  • Networking with top students and mentors from around the world

Award Ceremonies & Recognition

MU20 features major award categories celebrating excellence in innovation, social impact, leadership, and teamwork. Students compete to prove their potential on an international stage.

A Truly Global Gathering

Every year, MU20 unites young changemakers from diverse backgrounds, empowering them to tackle real-world issues and shape the leaders of tomorrow.
